Increasing access to health care in Anocastia doesn't lead to direct use | Dec. 8, 2021
  • After covering the opening of a new, full service health care center located in underserved Wards 7 and 8, I followed up with the center's director, a few of their doctors and several patients to understand why the brand new center was going unused. The answer: hesitancy from a population that is largely underserved and therefore skeptical of medicine.
